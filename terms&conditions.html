Complete Terms and Conditions for Applicants

Effective Date: Upon system deployment | Last Updated: September 2025

  1. Acceptance of Terms
    By registering and using PESO SmartHire, you agree to follow these guidelines. If you do not agree, do not use the system.

  2. System Purpose
    PESO SmartHire helps job seekers apply for employment opportunities through the Public Employment Service Office (PESO) of Lipa City.

  3. Registration Requirements
    • Provide accurate personal information and valid IDs.
    • Keep contact information up to date.
    • Create a secure password and keep login credentials confidential.

  4. Application Process
    • Submit truthful and complete applications.
    • Update your profile regularly.
    • Respond promptly to interview requests.
    • Attend scheduled appointments and interviews on time.

  5. Prohibited Activities
    • Creating multiple accounts with false information.
    • Submitting fraudulent documents.
    • Harassing employers or PESO staff.
    • Using the system for purposes other than job seeking.

  6. Data Privacy
    Your personal information is collected, encrypted, and stored securely. Data may be shared with approved employers for legitimate recruitment purposes or with DOLE for reporting. You are responsible for keeping your login credentials confidential.

  7. Account Suspension and Appeals
    Accounts may be suspended for violations such as providing false information or misusing the system. Appeals can be made by contacting PESO Lipa City with supporting documents.

  8. System Limitations
    The system only manages applications and recruitment facilitation. Walk-in applicants will receive staff-assisted encoding support.
