Bridging Applicants and Employers with Trust

PESO SmartHire is the official online platform of PESO Lipa designed to make the hiring process faster, smarter, and more transparent.

Whether you are a job seeker looking for opportunities or an employer in need of qualified talent, SmartHire connects you directly through a secure and trusted system.

About Us

The Public Employment Service Office (PESO) is a non-fee charging multi-dimensional employment service facility or entity established in all Local Government Units (is) in coordination with the Department of Labor and Employment (DOLE) pursuant to R.A. No 8799 or the PESO Act of 1999 as amended by R.A. No. 10691.

Our Mission

To synergize the worker-employer efforts toward economic development of the City by ensuring prompt, timely and efficient employment facilitation services.

Our Vision

To offer the most dependable and sustainable employment facilitation services that significantly contributes to poverty alleviation and economic development of the City.

Service Pledge

We commit to:

P

Provide the best venue in the City where people could simultaneously explore various employment options and other opportunities.

E

Ensure prompt and efficient delivery of employment facilitation services while delivering timely information on Labor Market and DOLE Programs.

S

Support the local government in achieving its objectives of Empowering people, Better governance and Advance services.

O

Offer holistic strategies, programs and activities of a modern Public Employment Service Office that delivers multi-dimensional employment facilitation services while serving the public with utmost care and commmitment.
