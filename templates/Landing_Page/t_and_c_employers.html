Complete Terms and Conditions for Employers

Effective Date: Upon system deployment | Last Updated: September 2025

1. Acceptance of Terms

By registering and using PESO SmartHire, you agree to follow these guidelines. If you do not agree, do not use the system.

2. System Purpose

PESO SmartHire helps employers post job vacancies and manage applicant submissions through the Public Employment Service Office (PESO) of Lipa City.

3. Registration Requirements

  • Provide valid company registration and business permits..
  • Submit accurate company information and contact details.
  • Designate authorized personnel to manage the account.
  • Keep company profile and job postings up to date.

4. Job Posting Requirements

  • Post only legitimate job opportunities.
  • Provide clear job descriptions, requirements, and compensation.
  • Update job status promptly when positions are filled.
  • Comply with labor laws and anti-discrimination policies.

5. Applicant Management

  • Review applications fairly and without discrimination.
  • Provide timely feedback to qualified applicants.
  • Conduct professional interviews and assessments.
  • Report hiring outcomes to PESO for record-keeping.

6. Data Privacy

Personal information of applicants is protected and shared only for recruitment purposes. Maintain confidentiality of applicant data.

7. Account Suspension

Accounts may be suspended for violations such as providing false information or misusing the system.

8. System Limitations

The system is limited to recruitment facilitation and applicant management. Payroll or HR management features are not included.
